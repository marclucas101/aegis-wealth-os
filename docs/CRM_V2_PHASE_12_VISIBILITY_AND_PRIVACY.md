# CRM V2 Phase 12 — Visibility and Privacy

---

## Reports privacy

- List-level safe counts only
- No NRIC, policy numbers, premiums, wealth, ethnicity
- No advocacy score in cards or ordering
- No full communication bodies
- No raw Google provider errors
- Trend direction is informational only — not sales priority

## Operations privacy

- Feature keys and boolean flags only
- No tokens, secrets, connection strings
- No raw migration credentials
- No raw provider payloads
- No protection document content in extraction panels
- Pilot allowlist size may be shown — not user IDs

## Aggregate operations views

No sensitive client data in book-wide aggregate operations views. Adviser-scoped counts only for advisor role.

## Accessibility

- Semantic section headings with `aria-labelledby`
- Status conveyed with text labels — not color-only
- Visible keyboard focus states
- Mobile-first layout without required horizontal scroll
